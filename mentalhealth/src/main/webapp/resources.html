Mental Health Resources

Below is a list of valuable resources to help you with various aspects of mental health. These resources range from articles and self-help tools to hotlines and professional support services.

  • National Institute of Mental Health (NIMH)
  • MentalHealth.gov
  • Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services Administration (SAMHSA)
  • BetterHelp - Online Therapy
  • Crisis Text Line
  • Headspace - Meditation and Mindfulness
  • 7 Cups - Free Emotional Support

If you or someone you know is in immediate danger, please contact local emergency services or reach out to the nearest mental health professional.
